Privacy Policy for Molting Mate

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Introduction

Molting Mate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Molting Mate (the "App").

Data We Collect

Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Spider profiles (name, species, age, individual type)
  • Feeding records and schedules
  • Molting history
  • Photos of your spiders
  • Purchase documents (photos/PDFs)
  • Notification preferences

Permissions We Request

Camera Permission

  • Why we need it: To allow you to take photos of your spiders and their purchase documents
  • What we do: Photos are saved locally on your device and never uploaded to any server

Photo Library / Media Access

  • Why we need it: To allow you to select existing photos from your device gallery
  • What we do: Selected photos are stored locally and never uploaded to any server

Notifications Permission

  • Why we need it: To send you reminders about feeding schedules
  • What we do: Notifications are generated locally on your device

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No third-party data sharing

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete it through the app
  • You uninstall the app

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual records or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: Export your data for backup purposes (if implemented)

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All data is stored locally on the device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
